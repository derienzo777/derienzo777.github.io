Sr. Lead Product Designer, BILL
Back to hands-on craft by choice. Network & AR teams.

I started as an art director — learning how to make things clear, trustworthy, and worth someone's attention. That foundation turned out to be exactly right for designing financial products.

I co-founded two startups. Booklaunch helped authors build professional sales pages without needing a developer. Betterlance gave freelancers a CRM for growing their client base. Both were acquired by Invoice2go in 2021 — not because they were flashy, but because they solved real problems cleanly.

For five years at BILL I've worked at every level: senior IC, design manager, senior manager, and director. A few years ago I stepped back from the director track to return to hands-on craft — something I've done intentionally a few times in my career. I'm better at both for it.
