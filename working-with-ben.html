01 The pattern

At every level, the same things showed up: calm through change, high standards, and a habit of making things better around me.

My career at BILL started with quickly earning trust in ambiguity — integrating acquired products, building strong cross-functional relationships, and establishing design as a credible partner in new spaces. From there it expanded into people leadership, org-level quality transformation, and high-leverage strategic product work.

From FY22 forward, the pattern in my reviews is remarkably consistent: recognized for calm leadership through change, strong communication, deep customer advocacy, and a habit of making the environment around the work better — through clearer process, stronger teams, and higher standards.

Customer centricity
Pushing teams toward the real customer problem, grounding decisions in research, usability testing, and direct feedback — not opinion.
Ownership mindset
Stepping into ambiguous spaces, taking on new domains, and helping move complex initiatives forward even when resources are constrained.
High standards
Raising the bar for design quality, operationalizing clearer review practices, and making design thinking more rigorous and reusable across teams.
Authenticity
Building trust and psychological safety, coaching with empathy, bringing positive energy to teams, and helping people do their best work without ego.

03 AI & tools

I don't wait for AI to become obvious. I experiment early, find what's actually useful, and build with it.

I've been an early and active explorer of AI tools — not just for design tasks, but for building real things that solve real problems at work and at home. A few examples:

1
Executive buy-in videos for AI investment
Built rapid internal marketing videos to get executive support for AI investment in AR. Scripted, generated imagery, created video and motion effects, and cloned my own voice for the narration — without a production team.
Gemini MidJourney Runway ElevenLabs Suno Jitter
2
Customer feedback synthesis tool
Built a tool that scours all entry points for customer feedback — surveys, support Slack channels — and themes them automatically, cutting the time to synthesize signals across channels.
Custom build
3
Research synthesis gem
Built a Gemini Gem that reads NotebookLM research docs to synthesize customer interview transcripts into product insights — turning hours of raw transcript into structured input for design decisions.
Gemini NotebookLM
4
Performance logging agent
Created a Glean agent to solve the problem of keeping up with logging wins and work against job expectations — so performance conversations are informed by evidence, not memory.
Glean
5
Prototyping with AI-native tools
Regularly prototype and explore with Figma Make, VS Code, and Claude Code — using code as a design material for things that need real interaction to evaluate.
Figma Make VS Code Claude Code
On AI and design

The designers who will matter most in the next five years aren't the ones who use AI tools — it's the ones who build judgment about when and how to use them. That's what I'm developing.
